About Me

Beyond work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, solving complex problems, and collaborating with teams worldwide.

In 2024, I presented my research, “Financial Health Assessment for Households in Kenya”, at the prestigious IEEE ICT4DA Conference. This study leverages data analysis techniques to assess financial health, offering insights into how Kenyan households can improve their economic stability.

I’ve also worked on notable projects, including:

  • Kenyan Business Ideas & Investment Bot: A machine learning-powered chatbot built with React and Flask to assist entrepreneurs in identifying viable investments using Kenyan economic data.
  • Smart Storage System: A cloud-based storage platform designed for seamless data organization and retrieval, featuring secure user authentication.
