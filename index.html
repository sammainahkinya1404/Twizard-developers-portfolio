About Me

Machine Learning Engineer and Full-Stack Developer based in Kenya, specializing in Python and React.

I build intelligent systems that solve real-world problems—from ML-powered chatbots helping Kenyan entrepreneurs to deep learning models detecting gastrointestinal diseases. My stack combines Python (TensorFlow, Flask, Django) on the backend with React and Next.js on the frontend.

Currently pursuing my MSc in Computer Science, I've published 3 IEEE papers on topics ranging from medical image classification to financial health assessment and NLP-based topic clustering. I thrive at the intersection of research and practical implementation.
